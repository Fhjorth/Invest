Keld Lundberg Holm

Founder and owner of 360invest

More than 30 years of experience within investment management and advisory including sustainable investment and active ownership (ESG).

From 2009-2017 CEO of The Danish Finance Society and CFA Society Denmark and prior to this Chief Investment Officer at Nykredit Asset Management.

Positions at Danske Capital as Head of Asset Allocation & Risk Management and Head of Retail & Development. Before working at Danske Capital, he was Chief Investment Officer at BG Bank Asset Management and Head of Research at Bikuben.
